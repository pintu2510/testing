  • What’s a Free Range chicken?
    A free range chicken has access to the outdoors for all or part of the day as compared to spending all its life inside a factory farm in pens and coops. Raising poultry as free range ensures that the chickens grow in a natural, stress-free environment on a natural, vegetarian diet. In order for any chicken to be organic, it needs to be free range. However, chickens raised in state-of-the-art growout houses aren’t necessarily less healthy than free range chickens. In fact, under humane supervision, both are a great and healthy source of meat.
